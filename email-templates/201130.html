Advent of Code 1-25 Dec

Every december something amazing happens. Programmers voluntarily wake up at 6.00am* and solve small cozy problems in Advent of Code. Christmas is about giving gifts, that is why LiTHe kod is donating 20 sek for every solved problem (gold star): 10 sek to COVID-19 research and 10 sek to Advent of Code itself. If you do well there is also a chance to win prizes! More info can be found at our website. This competition is made possible by Mindroad!

There will of course be a kickoff for Advent of Code at the meetup the first of December!

It's really fun!
- (at least) one participant from last year

* Not required to participate.
